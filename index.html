Our classes

The Batch of Five

1 : 5
teacher to students, every class

A one-to-five ratio is a genuine luxury — one most coaching simply can't offer.

True mentorship can't happen in a crowded room. We cap every batch at five students, which we've found is the balance point: each child gets real individual attention, while still learning alongside peers. When the group meets a common challenge, we work through it together; when one student is stuck on a concept, we pivot and give them the focused attention to master it.

We build our own curriculum and practice tests, with rigorous review and correction at every step.

Our story

Santona Academy grew from a lifelong love of mathematics — the belief that math isn't just a subject to get through, but something closer to magic: logic, structure, and a language worth cherishing. Too many children learn to fear it far too early, long before they ever meet its joy. We started Santona Academy to turn that fear into fascination.

The academy is named after our founder's late mother Santona Pathak, a lifelong passionate educator.

Santona means solace: calm, and a gentle space to grow.

That is the spirit we try to teach in — patient, personal, and unafraid. It is, quite simply, our way of giving something back.
